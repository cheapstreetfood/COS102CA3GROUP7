Our App is meant to connect people across the world through social connection, messaging and finding people on a feed
The app works by making a safe place to discover people using posts about their everyday lives and culture to connect with people.





Figma Link:https://www.figma.com/design/MlEmbom7Nm6R8aITwvoauE/COS-PROJECT?node-id=3399-25&t=rVCtqjHeBiNxadXo-0

Note:
-Try opening app from running navigation code
-Make sure pillow and tkinter and socket are installed on your device
-For Connecting to Client be sure the client has the same ip as host server
-Connection must be made over router.
-If encountering any problems. Check with your vpns or firewall that could possibly block connection on server side

Install Requirements
 -Pillow
 -Any build assistant
 -Setup a virtual environment if you must
